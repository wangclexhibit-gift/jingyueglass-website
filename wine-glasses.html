FAQ
Common Questions
What is the minimum order quantity for wine glasses?
Our MOQ is 30 pcs per style. You can mix multiple styles in one order — for example, 30 pcs red wine + 30 pcs champagne flutes can be shipped together. For OEM custom mold orders, MOQ is 500 pcs.
How long does it take to get a sample?
Standard in-stock samples are shipped within 1–2 business days. Custom samples (new shape or decoration) take 3–5 business days. Sample fees are fully refunded on your first production order.
Can I put my own logo on the wine glasses?
Yes. We support screen printing, decal transfer, sandblasting, gold rim, and color spraying. Please send your logo file (AI, PDF, or PNG) and we'll provide a digital mockup within 24 hours.
Do you provide FDA and LFGB certifications?
Yes, all our wine glasses are tested and certified to FDA (US) and LFGB (EU) food-contact safety standards. SGS and other third-party test reports can be arranged on request.
What are the payment terms?
Standard: 30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment. For long-term partners we offer flexible terms including L/C and net payment. We accept T/T, PayPal, and trade assurance.
Do you ship to South America and Southeast Asia?
Yes — we regularly ship to Brazil, Colombia, Argentina, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, and across Europe. We can arrange FCL/LCL sea freight, air express, and provide all export documents.
